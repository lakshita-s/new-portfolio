ABOUT

hello! I'm Lakshita! I'm a Design & Technology student at Parsons. I'm interested in interactive experiences, and emotional, empathetic and highly intentional design. I like to use philosophical themes in my artistic expression, and generally create from a place of personal experience, or an evaluation of the human condition. I'm very new to the world of coding, but I've always enjoyed niche internet surfing and playing video games; the few non-grandma hobbies i have (see: embroidering, baking, "taking walks").

This portfolio gathers the projects from my CC courses of sophomore year: Critical Computation Lab (Fall 25’) and Advanced Critical Computation (Spring 26’).
